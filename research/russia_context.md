# Russian Cultural & Tech Context (2000s-2010s)

## The Runet (Russian Internet)
- Developed somewhat independently due to language barrier.
- Key players: Yandex (Search), Mail.ru (Email/Portal), VK (Social).
- Culture: High piracy (torrents, mp3s), freedom of speech zone vs. state TV control.

## St. Petersburg vs. Moscow
- **St. Petersburg**: Intellectual capital, "window to Europe", gloomy, artistic, home of SPbGU and Durov. Singer House (Dom Knigi) is the iconic VK office.
- **Moscow**: Money, politics, oligarchs, flashy displays of wealth.

## Key Terms/Slang
- **Major (Мажор)**: Rich kid, usually with unearned privilege.
- **Gopnik (Гопник)**: Street hooligan, tracksuit-wearing lower class.
- **Botan (Ботан)**: Nerd, geek, bookworm (Durov was proud of this).
- **Vatnik (Ватник)**: Jingoistic patriot (later term, but roots present).
- **KGB/FSB**: Security services, always looming threat to privacy.

## Political Climate
- **Medvedev Thaw (2008-2012)**: Hope for modernization, "Skolkovo", tech-friendly atmosphere.
- **2011 Protests**: Bolotnaya Square protests against election fraud. Internet played key organizing role.
- **Putin's Return (2012+)**: Tightening of screws, censorship laws, pressure on internet giants.
