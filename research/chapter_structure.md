# Код Дурова - Chapter Structure

## Book Information
- **Title (Russian)**: Код Дурова. Реальная история «ВКонтакте» и ее создателя
- **Title (English)**: Durov Code. The Real Story of VKontakte and Its Creator
- **Author**: Николай В. Кононов (Nikolai V. Kononov)
- **Publisher**: Манн, Иванов, Фербер (Mann, Ivanov, Ferber)
- **Year**: 2013
- **ISBN**: 978-5-91657-546-0
- **Total Pages**: 99

## Chapter Structure

### Front Matter (Pages 1-4)
| Page | Content |
|------|---------|
| 1 | Title page - Author name, book title |
| 2 | Publisher info, ISBN, annotation |
| 3 | Table of Contents (Содержание) |
| 4 | Complementary book recommendations |

### Предисловие / Preface (Pages 5-6)
- **Author**: Юрий Сапрыкин (Yuri Saprykin)
- **Role**: Публицист, шеф-редактор «Рамблер-Афиша» (Publicist, Chief Editor of Rambler-Afisha)
- **Content**: Sets the context for why this book matters, discusses Russia's "lost narratives" and Durov as an exceptional figure

### Пролог / Prologue (Pages 7-12)
- **Setting**: VKontakte office in the Singer building, St. Petersburg
- **Scene**: Negotiations with a foreign retail company executive
- **Purpose**: Introduces Durov's negotiation style, eccentricity, and the company culture
- **Key Characters Introduced**:
  - Pavel Durov (основатель / founder)
  - The "white-haired programmer" (likely Nikolai Durov)
  - Foreign retail executive (unnamed)

### Глава 1 / Chapter 1 (Pages 13-22)
- **Theme**: Childhood and Background
- **Content**: Durov family history, childhood in Leningrad/St. Petersburg, father Valery Durov (professor), brother Nikolai
- **Key Topics**: Education, intellectual upbringing, early influences

### Глава 2 / Chapter 2 (Pages 23-37)
- **Theme**: University Years
- **Content**: Studies at St. Petersburg State University, early projects, student activism
- **Key Topics**: Academic achievements, first programming projects, developing worldview

### Глава 3 / Chapter 3 (Pages 38-50)
- **Theme**: VKontakte Founding
- **Content**: Creation of VKontakte in 2006, early development, initial growth
- **Key Topics**: Technical decisions, founding team, first users

### Глава 4 / Chapter 4 (Pages 51-64)
- **Theme**: Growth and Scaling
- **Content**: Rapid user acquisition, technical challenges, team building
- **Key Topics**: Infrastructure scaling, hiring, competition

### Глава 5 / Chapter 5 (Pages 65-78)
- **Theme**: Business Conflicts
- **Content**: Investor relations, ownership disputes, business pressures
- **Key Topics**: Mail.ru, Usmanov, corporate battles

### Глава 6 / Chapter 6 (Pages 79-91)
- **Theme**: Philosophy and Maturity
- **Content**: Durov's worldview, libertarian philosophy, vision for VK
- **Key Topics**: Freedom, anti-censorship stance, personal philosophy

### Глава 7 / Chapter 7 (Pages 92-98)
- **Theme**: Future Outlook
- **Content**: Where VK is headed, Durov's future plans
- **Key Topics**: Legacy, potential developments

### Об авторе / About the Author (Page 99)
- **Content**: Information about author Nikolai Kononov
- **Background**: Journalist, researcher

## Key Recurring Themes

1. **Innovation vs. Tradition**: Durov's unconventional approach to business
2. **Russian Tech Ecosystem**: Context of building tech in Russia
3. **Youth and Disruption**: Young founders challenging establishment
4. **Freedom and Control**: Tensions between growth and independence
5. **Code as Philosophy**: Technical decisions reflecting worldview

## Translation Notes

- The book mixes formal business language with informal tech slang
- Contains numerous references to Russian internet culture (RuNet)
- Names should be transliterated consistently
- Technical terms should match industry standards in each target language
