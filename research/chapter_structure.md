## Key Themes Across Chapters

1. **Individual vs System**: Durov's resistance to conventional business/social norms
2. **Technical Excellence**: Emphasis on code quality and programmer culture
3. **Russian Context**: Post-Soviet entrepreneurship, business environment
4. **Growth Story**: From dorm room to 20M+ users in ~6 years
5. **Personality**: Multiple facets of Durov - genius, provocateur, philosopher
6. **Control**: Maintaining independence from investors and state

## Special Considerations for Translation

- **Prologue is critical**: Sets narrative voice, introduces main character, establishes tone
- **Chapters 3-5 most complex**: Heavy on business/tech terminology
- **Chapter 6 philosophical**: Requires careful translation of abstract concepts
- **Cultural references throughout**: Russian places, people, events need research
- **Durov's voice**: Must preserve sharp, provocative, unconventional personality
