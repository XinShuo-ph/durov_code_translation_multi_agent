# Durov Code Book - Chapter Structure

## Book Information
- **Title (Russian)**: Код Дурова. Реальная история «ВКонтакте» и ее создателя
- **Title (English)**: Durov's Code. The Real Story of "VKontakte" and Its Creator
- **Author**: Николай В. Кононов (Nikolai V. Kononov)
- **Publisher**: Манн, Иванов, Фербер; Москва; 2013
- **ISBN**: 978-5-91657-546-0
- **Total Pages**: 99

---

## Chapter Structure

### Pages 1-4: Front Matter
- Title page
- Publisher information
- ISBN
- Аннотация (Annotation/Summary)
- Содержание (Table of Contents)
- Additional book recommendations

### Pages 5-6: Предисловие (Preface)
- **Author**: Юрий Сапрыкин (Yuri Saprykin), публицист, шеф-редактор «Рамблер-Афиша»
- **Content**: Introduction to the book, discussing Russia as a country of "lost stories," the phenomenon of VKontakte, and Pavel Durov as an exceptional figure who defies expectations
- **Key Themes**: Russian tech entrepreneurship, VKontakte as a cultural phenomenon, Durov as unconventional figure

### Pages 7-12: Пролог (Prologue)
- **Location**: Singer House (Дом Зингера), St. Petersburg, 6th floor - VKontakte offices
- **Content**: 
  - Vivid scene of business negotiations between VKontakte and an e-commerce company
  - Introduction to Pavel Durov's negotiating style and personality
  - Description of VKontakte's office culture and the "nerds" who work there
  - Author's reflection on how technology and social networks have changed human consciousness
  - Author's journey to write this book
- **Key Characters Introduced**: Pavel Durov, VKontakte programmers
- **Key Themes**: Nerds vs. business world, social networks as collective nervous system, Durov's unconventional personality

### Pages 13-22: Глава 1 - Ботанический сад (Chapter 1 - Botanical Garden)
- **Content**: 
  - Durov's childhood in St. Petersburg's outskirts
  - Description of the neighborhood (Приморский район - Primorsky district)
  - Family background: Father Valery Durov (philologist), mother, brothers Nikolai (mathematician) and Mikhail
  - Time spent in Italy (father taught at Turin University)
  - Return to Russia
  - Early intellectual development
- **Key Themes**: Childhood, family influences, formative environment

### Pages 23-37: Глава 2 (Chapter 2) - University Years
- **Content**: 
  - Durov's university years at St. Petersburg State University
  - Early projects and web development
  - Meeting key future collaborators
  - Development of programming skills
- **Key Themes**: Education, early internet projects, building networks

### Pages 38-50: Глава 3 (Chapter 3) - VKontakte Founding
- **Content**: 
  - The founding of VKontakte in 2006
  - Early development and launch
  - Initial user growth
  - Technical challenges and solutions
- **Key Themes**: Startup founding, product development, early success

### Pages 51-64: Глава 4 (Chapter 4) - Growth and Scaling
- **Content**: 
  - Rapid user growth
  - Building the team
  - Technical infrastructure scaling
  - Business model development
- **Key Themes**: Scaling, team building, technical challenges

### Pages 65-78: Глава 5 (Chapter 5) - Business Conflicts
- **Content**: 
  - Investor relations
  - Business disputes
  - Competition with other platforms
  - Pressure from various parties
- **Key Themes**: Corporate conflicts, investor relations, independence

### Pages 79-91: Глава 6 (Chapter 6) - Philosophy and Worldview
- **Content**: 
  - Durov's personal philosophy
  - Views on technology and society
  - Leadership style
  - Future vision
- **Key Themes**: Philosophy, libertarian views, technology ideology

### Pages 92-98: Глава 7 (Chapter 7) - Future Outlook
- **Content**: 
  - Future of VKontakte
  - Durov's legacy
  - Predictions and possibilities
- **Key Themes**: Future, legacy, vision

### Page 99: Об авторе (About the Author)
- Information about Nikolai Kononov

---

## Key Locations
1. **Дом Зингера (Singer House)** - VKontakte headquarters, Nevsky Prospekt, St. Petersburg
2. **St. Petersburg outskirts** - Durov's childhood neighborhood
3. **Turin, Italy** - Where the family lived during father's professorship
4. **St. Petersburg State University** - Durov's education

## Key Characters
1. **Pavel Durov** - Founder of VKontakte, main subject
2. **Nikolai Durov** - Older brother, mathematician, key technical mind
3. **Valery Durov** - Father, philology professor
4. **Vyacheslav Mirilashvili** - Early investor
5. **Various VKontakte team members**

## Translation Notes
- Book written before Durov left VKontakte (2014) and before Telegram era
- Covers period 2006-2012 primarily
- Heavy use of Russian internet slang and cultural references
- Contains many St. Petersburg-specific references
