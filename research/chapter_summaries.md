## Пролог / Prologue (Pages 7-12)
**Title**: [No chapter title - just "Prologue"]
**Setting**: VKontakte office, 6th floor of Singer Building, Nevsky Prospekt, St. Petersburg

**Key Scene**: Business negotiation over e-commerce partnership ("Желания"/Desires feature)

**Characters Introduced**:
- **Foreign executive**: E-commerce company director (expat living in Russia)
- **"Attack dog" negotiator**: Tough negotiator at window
- **Blonde developer**: Main developer with laptop (Nikolai Durov implied)
- **Pavel Durov**: "Unremarkable guy in baseball cap" who suddenly explodes with demand for 20% commission

**Key Moments**:
- Three-hour negotiation over commission rates
- Foreign executive offers 5%, VK wants 10%
- Suddenly the quiet guy in baseball cap (Durov) erupts: "20 percent or we don't work with you!"
- Establishes Durov's unconventional, unpredictable negotiation style
- Shows VK's power position (20 million daily users)

**Purpose**: Sets tone for Durov's character - can be quiet then suddenly forceful, unconventional businessman

## Глава 1 / Chapter 1: Ботанический сад / Botanical Garden (Pages 13-22)

**Key Topics**:
- **Durov's childhood** in panel housing district of St. Petersburg
- **Physical setting**: Post-Soviet urban landscape description
  - Panel housing (панельные дома)
  - Empty fields, high-voltage towers, wind from the sea
  - Unfinished construction, gopniks, muddy yards
  - Journey from home to Botanical Garden

**Character Development**:
- Young Durov reading Cervantes (Don Quixote)
- Scanning for gopniks (street toughs) - class/danger awareness
- Intellectual interests contrasting with rough neighborhood
- Father's academic background (philology professor)

**Symbolic Elements**:
- Don Quixote reference: Durov as idealist/dreamer
- Botanical Garden: Place of learning, escape from rough neighborhood
- Architecture of inequality: Orange/burgundy panels, Soviet planning
- Journey motif: Physical and intellectual journey from origins

**Cultural Context**:
- Post-Soviet St. Petersburg
- Class stratification in housing
- Academic family in working-class neighborhood
- 1990s Russia childhood experience

## Глава 3 / Chapter 3: Мафия открывает глаза / Mafia Opens Its Eyes (Pages 38-50)

- **VKontakte founding** (October 10, 2006)
- **Early investment**: Mirilashvili becomes key early investor/partner
- **Business structure**: Partnership between Durov's vision and Mirilashvili's business acumen
- **Growth strategy**: Expanding beyond university students
- **Technical development**: Building the platform

**Business Context**:
- Post-crisis Russia (2000s boom)
- Consumer market growing
- Internet penetration increasing
- Competition with Odnoklassniki, attempts by Mail.ru

**Tension Seeds**:
- Different worldviews: Durov (idealist) vs. investors (profit-focused)
- Control questions emerging

- **Business conflicts** intensifying

- Protecting walls: Defending user privacy

**Tension Escalation**:
- Investors want more control
- Government wants cooperation
- Durov wants independence
- Money vs. principles conflict

**Cultural Context**:
- Russian business practices (informal, personal)
- Government-business relations
- Oligarch influence
- Durov's unusual principled stance

## Cross-Chapter Themes

### Arc of Rise and Conflict
1. **Childhood** (Ch 1): Origins, intellectual formation
2. **University** (Ch 2): Finding collaborators, early experiments
3. **Founding** (Ch 3): Creating VKontakte, early success
4. **Growth** (Ch 4): Scaling challenges, technical excellence
5. **Conflict** (Ch 5): Battles with investors, government
6. **Philosophy** (Ch 6): Maturity, worldview development
7. **Future** (Ch 7): What's next, legacy thinking

### Recurring Motifs
- **Don Quixote**: Idealist fighting windmills (establishment, conventions)
- **Architecture/Building**: Durov as architect of social spaces
- **Games**: Olymp, Mafia, racing games - life as game/strategy
- **Night work**: Unconventional hours, intense dedication
- **St. Petersburg**: City as character, Imperial vs. Soviet
- **Freedom vs. Control**: Central tension throughout

### Character Evolution
- **Early Durov**: Quiet, intellectual, experimental
- **Middle Durov**: Builder, leader, growing confidence
- **Later Durov**: Philosopher, provocateur, principled rebel

### Business Tension
- **Idealism vs. Pragmatism**: Durov's vision vs. investors' profit focus
- **Freedom vs. Control**: User privacy vs. government demands
- **Local vs. Global**: Russian success vs. international ambitions
