# History of VKontakte (VK)

## Founding (2006)
- Launch: October 10, 2006 (Durov's 22nd birthday).
- Beta: Invite-only for SPbGU students.
- Name: "VKontakte" ("InContact") - chosen to not limit to universities.

## Growth (2007-2010)
- 2007: became second largest social network in Russia (overtaking Odnoklassniki).
- Technology: Written in PHP, then heavily optimized with C++ (KPHP) by Nikolai Durov.
- Features: Wall, messaging, audio/video (piracy controversy), clean UI.

## The Corporate War (2011-2014)
- **Ownership structure**: Originally Durov + Mirilashvili + Leviev.
- **Mail.ru Group**: Yuri Milner's DST bought stake, gradually increased to 39.99%.
- **2013**: Mirilashvili and Leviev sold 48% to UCP (United Capital Partners) - a hostile entity linked to Sechin/Kremlin.
- **2014**: Durov sold his 12% to Ivan Tavrin (MegaFon), who sold to Mail.ru. Mail.ru bought UCP stake, becoming 100% owner.

## Key Events
- **2011 Duma Elections**: FSB demanded blocking of Navalny's groups. Durov posted picture of dog in hoodie with tongue out.
- **2012 Money Drop**: Durov threw paper planes made of 5000-ruble notes from Singer House office window.
- **2013 Road Accident**: Accusations of Durov running over a policeman (widely seen as fabrication to pressure him).
