# Color + font scheme proposal (RU/EN/ZH/JA)

Goal: keep 4 languages readable in parallel, with strong but not neon color cues.

## Colors (hex)

| Language | Color | Hex | Notes |
|----------|-------|-----|------|
| Russian (RU) | black | `#000000` | baseline |
| English (EN) | blue | `#1E5AA8` | calm “link-like” blue; high contrast |
| Chinese (ZH) | red | `#B01E1E` | muted red to avoid glare |
| Japanese (JA) | green | `#167A3A` | darker green for contrast |

## Fonts

- **Latin/Cyrillic**: `Noto Serif` (main), `Noto Sans` (optional UI labels)
- **Chinese**: `Noto Serif CJK SC`
- **Japanese**: `Noto Serif CJK JP`

Rationale: Noto family has broad Unicode coverage and consistent weight/metrics across scripts, reducing layout surprises.
