# Pavel Durov Biography

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Pavel Valerievich Durov (Павел Валерьевич Дуров)
- **Born**: October 10, 1984
- **Birthplace**: Leningrad (now St. Petersburg), USSR
- **Nationality**: Russian (now UAE resident, French citizenship)
- **Known For**: Founder of VKontakte (VK), Founder and CEO of Telegram

---

## Family Background

### Father: Valery Semyonovich Durov
- Professor of philology at St. Petersburg State University
- Specialist in Latin language and Roman literature
- Author of academic works on classical languages
- Significant influence on Pavel's intellectual development

### Mother
- Academic background
- Supported children's education

### Brother: Nikolai Durov
- Born 1980 (4 years older than Pavel)
- Mathematical prodigy, International Mathematical Olympiad gold medalist
- PhD in mathematics
- Key technical architect behind VKontakte and later Telegram
- Described as the "brains" behind the technical infrastructure
- Notoriously private, rarely appears publicly

---

## Early Life (1984-2001)

### Childhood in St. Petersburg
- Grew up in Kupchino district (southern outskirts of St. Petersburg)
- Later moved to different districts
- Spent formative years (ages 4-11) in Turin, Italy (father on academic assignment)
- Became fluent in Italian
- Returned to Russia in mid-1990s

### Education
- Attended Academic Gymnasium at St. Petersburg State University
- Showed early aptitude for languages and programming
- Influenced by brother Nikolai's mathematical abilities
- Read voraciously (Cervantes, philosophy, literature)

### Early Character Traits
- Independent thinker from young age
- Resistant to authority
- Interested in technology and internet early
- Known for sharp intellect and unconventional views

---

## University Years (2001-2006)

### St. Petersburg State University
- Faculty of Philology (like his father)
- Specialized in linguistics
- Graduated with honors

### Early Online Projects
- Created **durov.com** forum (2001) - student discussion platform
- Forum became popular among St. Petersburg students
- Learned web development and community management
- Built reputation as online community leader

### Key Skills Developed
- Web programming (PHP, MySQL)
- User interface design
- Community management
- Understanding of viral growth

---

## VKontakte Era (2006-2014)

### Founding VK (2006)
- **Launch Date**: October 10, 2006 (his 22nd birthday)
- Inspired by Facebook (which he encountered in 2005-2006)
- Adapted concept for Russian market
- Initial name: "Студенты.ру" (Students.ru), renamed to VKontakte ("In Contact")

### Rapid Growth
- 2007: 1 million users
- 2008: 10 million users
- 2010: 50 million users
- 2012: 100+ million users
- Became largest social network in Russia and CIS

### Leadership Style
- Hands-on involvement in product decisions
- Known for working late nights
- Unconventional office culture (open space, minimal hierarchy)
- Famous for provocative public stunts
- Cultivated image of tech rebel

### Key Conflicts
- **Copyright Issues**: VK allowed music/video sharing, faced legal pressure
- **Government Relations**: Refused to censor opposition content
- **Investor Disputes**: Conflicts with Mail.ru Group shareholders

### Departure from VK
- 2014: Sold remaining stake, left company
- Claimed he was forced out due to refusing government demands
- Left Russia, became "digital nomad"

---

## Telegram Era (2013-Present)

### Founding Telegram
- **Launched**: August 2013 (initially iOS only)
- Co-founded with brother Nikolai
- Focus on security, privacy, speed
- Self-funded, no external investors initially

### Telegram Growth
- 2016: 100 million users
- 2018: 200 million users
- 2021: 500 million users
- 2024: 900+ million users

### Key Features
- End-to-end encryption (Secret Chats)
- Channels and large groups
- Bots platform
- Cloud-based (unlike WhatsApp)

### Legal Battles
- Russia attempted to block Telegram (2018-2020)
- Durov refused to provide encryption keys
- Eventually unblocked in 2020

## Relevance to Book

The biography "Код Дурова" (Durov Code) by Nikolai Kononov was written in 2012-2013, covering:
- Childhood and family background
- University years and early projects
- VKontakte founding and growth (2006-2012)
