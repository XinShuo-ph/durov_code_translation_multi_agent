# Pavel Durov Biography - Research Notes

## Basic Information

**Full Name**: Pavel Valeryevich Durov (Павел Валерьевич Дуров)

**Birth**: October 10, 1984, in Leningrad (now St. Petersburg), USSR

**Current Status** (as of book publication ~2012-2013):
- Founder and CEO of VKontakte (VK.com)
- Age: ~28 years old
- Living in St. Petersburg, Russia

**Known Facts** (by 2024, post-book):

- Founded Telegram messenger
- Net worth: ~$15+ billion (Forbes estimates)
- Living in Dubai/international

## VKontakte Founding (2006)

### Launch: October 10, 2006 (his 22nd birthday)
- Initially called "VKontakte" (В Контакте = "In Contact")
- Inspired by Facebook but adapted for Russian market
- Coded primarily by Nikolai Durov
- Pavel handled business, product vision, marketing

### Early Strategy
- **Target**: University students first (like Facebook)
- **Features**: Profile pages, messaging, groups, media sharing
- **Differentiation**: Better suited for Cyrillic, Russian user behavior
- **Growth**: Viral spread among Russian-speaking youth

### Key Innovation
- Allowed sharing of pirated content (music, video)
- More permissive than Facebook
- Privacy-focused (initially)
- No ads (initially)
