# Pavel Durov Biography

## Basic Information
- **Name**: Pavel Valeryevich Durov (Павел Валерьевич Дуров)
- **Born**: October 10, 1984, Leningrad (now St. Petersburg), USSR
- **Nationality**: Russian, Saint Kitts and Nevis, French, UAE
- **Known as**: Founder of VKontakte (VK), Founder of Telegram
- **Brother**: Nikolai Durov (Mathematician, Lead Dev/CTO of VK and Telegram)

## Early Life
- **Family**: Son of Valery Durov, a philologist and head of the classical philology department at St. Petersburg State University (SPbGU).
- **Childhood**: Spent part of his childhood in Turin, Italy, where his father worked.
- **Education**: Attended the Academic Gymnasium in St. Petersburg. Graduated from the Philology Department of St. Petersburg State University (English Philology and Translation) in 2006.
- **Personality**: Known for being libertarian, eccentric, vegetarian, and wearing mostly black.

## Pre-VK Projects
- **Durov.com**: A library of materials for students of the humanities faculty.
- **Spbgu.ru**: A forum for university students. This was the testing ground for many features later implemented in VK (profiles, rating systems). He was known for creating fake accounts to stimulate discussion (a tactic later mentioned in *The Durov Code*).

## VKontakte Era (2006-2014)
- **Founding**: Launched VKontakte in October 2006, shortly after graduation. Inspired by Facebook but adapted for the Russian market.
- **Role**: CEO and main ideologue.
- **Philosophy**: Focused on speed, clean UI, and freedom of speech (often leading to conflicts over copyright and extremism).
- **Conflict**: Clashed with mail.ru Group (investors) who wanted to monetize more aggressively and integrate VK into their holding.
- **Political Pressure**: Refused to block opposition groups (e.g., Navalny's) during 2011 protests.
- **Exit**: "Sold" his stake in VK in early 2014 under pressure. Dismissed as CEO in April 2014. Left Russia.

## Telegram Era (2013-Present)
- **Founding**: Launched Telegram in 2013 as a secure messaging app, initially funded by his "Digital Fortress" fund.
- **Encryption**: Emphasized privacy and MTProto encryption (designed by Nikolai Durov).
- **Nomadic Life**: Traveled the world with a small team of developers, avoiding legal jurisdictions.
- **TON Blockchain**: Attempted to launch TON (Telegram Open Network) and Gram cryptocurrency, blocked by US SEC in 2020.
- **Current Status**: Residing in Dubai. Telegram has over 900 million active users (as of 2024).

## Key Traits & Views
- **Libertarianism**: Strong believer in free markets and minimal state intervention.
- **Privacy**: Believes privacy is a fundamental right.
- **Lifestyle**: Teetotaler, vegetarian, fitness enthusiast.
- **"Code of Durov"**: The book by Nikolai Kononov focuses on his worldview: "Totem" (leadership), rigid principles, and the belief that code can change the world.
