## Family

### Father: Valery Durov (Валерий Семёнович Дуров)
- Professor of philology at St. Petersburg State University
- Specialist in ancient languages and Roman literature
- Strong influence on sons' intellectual development
- Published author on Latin literature

### Mother
- Academic background
- Supported children's education

### Brother: Nikolai Durov (Николай Валерьевич Дуров)
- Born: November 21, 1980
- Mathematician and programmer
- IMO gold medalist (4-time winner)
- ACM ICPC world champion
- Co-founder and lead developer of VKontakte
- Chief architect of Telegram's MTProto protocol
- Often described as the "technical genius" behind both platforms

## Education

### Early Education
- Spent early childhood in Turin, Italy (father was guest lecturer)
- Returned to St. Petersburg for schooling
- Attended prestigious school with emphasis on languages

### University
- **Institution**: St. Petersburg State University
- **Faculty**: Philology (like his father)
- **Years**: 2001-2006
- **Notable**: Founded VKontakte while still a student
- **Activities**: Student forum moderator, early web projects

## Philosophy and Worldview

### Core Beliefs
1. **Digital Freedom**: Strong advocate for privacy and free communication
2. **Anti-Censorship**: Repeatedly refused government demands to censor content
3. **Libertarianism**: Skeptical of government intervention
4. **Minimalism**: Known for ascetic lifestyle despite wealth
5. **Fitness**: Practices intermittent fasting, vegetarianism, no alcohol

### Famous Quotes
- "Telegram will continue to defend freedom and privacy."
- "I prefer freedom to money."
- On management: Prefers flat hierarchies, small teams

### Public Persona
- Rarely gives interviews
- Communicates through Telegram channel
- Known for provocative statements
- Compared to Steve Jobs for similar black turtleneck attire

## Controversies

### VKontakte Period
- Copyright infringement accusations from major labels
- Refusal to cooperate with Russian authorities
- Conflicts with investors (Usmanov, Mail.ru)

### Post-Russia
- Criticized by multiple governments
- Telegram blocked in Russia (2018-2020), Iran, China
- TON cryptocurrency project terminated due to SEC

### 2024 Arrest
- Detained in France (August 2024)
- Charges related to platform moderation
- International attention on tech CEO liability
