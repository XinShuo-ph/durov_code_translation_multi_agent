# Pavel Durov Biography

## Basic Information
- **Born**: October 10, 1984
- **Place of Birth**: Leningrad (St. Petersburg), Russia
- **Citizenship**: St. Kitts and Nevis, France, UAE, Russia (left 2014)

## Family
- **Father**: Valery Semenovich Durov, Doctor of Philological Sciences, author of many scientific papers, former head of the department of classical philology at St. Petersburg State University.
- **Mother**: Albina Durova.
- **Brother**: Nikolai Durov (born 1980), mathematician, programmer, two-time absolute world champion in programming. Key technical co-founder of VK and Telegram.

## Education
- **School**: Academic Gymnasium (St. Petersburg).
- **University**: St. Petersburg State University, Philological Faculty.
- **Specialization**: English Philology and Translation.
- **Military Training**: Specialized in Propaganda and Psychological Warfare.

## Career

### Early Projects
- **Durov.com**: A database of exam materials for humanities students.
- **Spbgu.ru**: A forum for St. Petersburg State University students, known for its freedom of speech and Durov's active participation (often using sockpuppets to spark debate).

### VKontakte (VK)
- **Founded**: October 10, 2006 (on his 22nd birthday).
- **Co-founders**: Vyacheslav Mirilashvili, Lev Leviev.
- **Role**: CEO and chief product architect.
- **Growth**: Rapidly became the most popular social network in Russia and CIS.
- **Conflict**: 2011 standoff with FSB regarding opposition groups; 2013 traffic incident; 2014 conflict with UCP fund and Mail.ru Group.
- **Exit**: Sold his stake in January 2014; fired from CEO position in April 2014; left Russia.

### Telegram
- **Founded**: 2013 (initially as a secure messenger project funded by Digital Fortress).
- **Philosophy**: Focus on privacy, encryption, and freedom from censorship.
- **Growth**: Reached 900M+ active users (as of 2024).
- **TON (The Open Network)**: Blockchain project initiated in 2018, later community-led after SEC conflict.

## Philosophy & Personality
- **Libertarian Views**: Strong advocate for freedom of speech and deregulation.
- **Lifestyle**: Vegetarian, teetotaler, ascetic lifestyle, wears mostly black.
- **"Code of Durov"**: The book describes him as a "totem", a charismatic leader who values meritocracy and technical excellence over bureaucracy.

## Key Quotes (from Book Context)
- "I want to become an Internet totem."
- "When you don't lie, you can't be manipulated."
