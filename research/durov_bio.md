# Pavel Durov Biography

## Basic Information
- **Born**: October 10, 1984
- **Place of Birth**: Leningrad (St. Petersburg), USSR
- **Education**: St. Petersburg State University (Philology Department), graduated with honors (2006)
- **Known As**: "Russian Mark Zuckerberg", Founder of VKontakte and Telegram

## Family Background
- **Father**: Valery Semenovich Durov, Doctor of Philological Sciences, author of many scientific papers, head of the department of classical philology at SPbGU.
- **Mother**: Albina Durova, teacher at SPbGU.
- **Brother**: Nikolai Durov (born 1980), mathematician, two-time absolute world champion in programming, key architect of VK and Telegram protocols (MTProto).

## Early Life & Education
- Spent childhood in Turin, Italy, due to father's work.
- Returned to Russia, attended the Academic Gymnasium.
- Studied English Philology and Translation at SPbGU.
- During university, created `durov.com` (library of exam answers) and `spbgu.ru` (university forum).
- Won Potanin scholarship, known for high intelligence and rebellious streak.

## VKontakte Era (2006-2014)
- **Founding**: Launched VKontakte in October 2006 (beta in summer 2006) with Vyacheslav Mirilashvili and Lev Leviev.
- **Role**: CEO and chief product architect.
- **Philosophy**: Minimalist design, speed, free speech, piracy (initially lax on copyright).
- **Conflict**: Resisted Mail.ru Group's attempts to take full control.
- **2011 Protests**: Refused FSB orders to block opposition groups, posted picture of a dog in hoodie with tongue out.
- **The "White House" Incident**: Threw paper planes made of 5,000 ruble notes from VK office window (Singer House) in 2012.
- **Departure**: Dismissed as CEO in April 2014 after refusing to hand over data on Ukrainian protesters. Left Russia.

## Telegram Era (2013-Present)
- **Founding**: Founded Telegram in 2013 with brother Nikolai. Focus on encryption and speed.
- **Digital Nomad**: Travelled the world with a small team of developers, avoiding legal jurisdictions.
- **TON**: Tried to launch TON (Telegram Open Network) blockchain, blocked by SEC in 2020.
- **Current Status**: CEO of Telegram, residing in Dubai. Citizen of St. Kitts and Nevis, France, UAE.

## Personality & Beliefs
- **Libertarian**: Strong believer in freedom of speech and privacy.
- **Vegetarian/Teetotaler**: Promotes healthy lifestyle, avoids alcohol, meat, caffeine.
- **Style**: Often wears all black (Neo from Matrix style).
- **Quotes**:
    - "Communication is overrated."
    - "To be truly free, you should be ready to risk everything for freedom."
