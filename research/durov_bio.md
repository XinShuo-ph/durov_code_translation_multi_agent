# Pavel Durov - Biography

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Павел Валерьевич Дуров (Pavel Valeryevich Durov)
- **Born**: October 10, 1984, Leningrad (now St. Petersburg), USSR
- **Nationality**: Russian (later multi-citizenship)
- **Known For**: Founder of VKontakte, Founder/CEO of Telegram

## Family Background

### Father
- **Name**: Valery Semyonovich Durov (Валерий Семёнович Дуров)
- **Profession**: Doctor of Philological Sciences, Professor
- **Specialty**: Classical philology, Latin literature
- **Career**: Taught at St. Petersburg State University, lectured at University of Turin (Italy)

### Mother
- Worked as a teacher

### Brothers
1. **Nikolai Durov** (Николай Дуров)
   - Older brother
   - Brilliant mathematician, programming prodigy
   - Read at age 3, solved cubic equations at age 7
   - Appeared on TV show for child prodigies
   - Key technical architect of VKontakte
   - Later, key developer at Telegram

2. **Mikhail Durov** (Михаил Дуров)
   - Older half-brother (from mother's previous relationship)
   - Lived separately

## Early Life

### Childhood Location
- Grew up in Приморский район (Primorsky district) of St. Petersburg
- Peripheral, newly-built neighborhood with unfinished construction
- Near the Gulf of Finland (Baltic Sea)

### Italy Period
- Family moved to Turin, Italy when father received professorship
- Lived there for several years during childhood
- Pavel attended Italian school
- Returned to Russia later

### Education
- Attended specialized academic gymnasium in St. Petersburg
- Known as excellent student but with behavioral issues ("отличник с двойкой по поведению")
- St. Petersburg State University - studied philology (like his father)
- Created student forum while at university

## Key Personality Traits (as described in the book)

### Intellectual Characteristics
- Voracious reader (Cervantes mentioned as early favorite)
- Strong philosophical bent
- Interested in libertarian ideas
- Anti-establishment thinking

### Social/Behavioral
- Unconventional, sometimes confrontational
- Strong sense of independence
- Capable of intense focus
- Sharp negotiating style (described as "attack dog")
- Multiple personas: awkward teenager, aggressive businessman, refined intellectual

### Distinctive Habits
- Often wore black clothing
- Black baseball cap (bейсболка)
- Minimalist aesthetic preferences

## Early Interests

### Reading
- Don Quixote by Cervantes (carried as a child)
- Philosophy
- Literature

### Technology
- Early interest in computers and programming
- Web development during university years
- Inspired by Silicon Valley tech culture

## Key Life Events (Pre-VKontakte)

1. **Birth in Leningrad** (1984)
2. **Move to Italy** (late 1980s/early 1990s)
3. **Return to St. Petersburg** (1990s)
4. **University Years** (early 2000s)
5. **Student Forum Creation** - Early web project
6. **VKontakte Founding** (October 10, 2006 - on his 22nd birthday)

## VKontakte Era (2006-2014)

### Founding
- Founded VKontakte on October 10, 2006 (his 22nd birthday)
- Modeled partly on Facebook but localized for Russian market
- Brother Nikolai provided key technical expertise

### Leadership
- CEO and public face of the company
- Known for unconventional management style
- Fiercely protected company independence
- Clashed with investors and authorities

### Notable Incidents (mentioned in book)
- Threw money from Singer House balcony
- Organized provocative photo shoots
- Posted controversial statements on Victory Day (May 9)
- Refused to limit opposition politician Navalny's group size
- Used Faust quote: "Vi veri universum vivus vici"

### Departure
- Lost control of VKontakte in 2014
- Sold shares to Mail.ru Group
- Left Russia

## Post-VKontakte (Context - after book period)

### Telegram
- Founded Telegram Messenger in 2013
- Left Russia in 2014
- Based in various countries (Berlin, Singapore, Dubai)
- Telegram grew to 700+ million users
- Known for strong encryption and privacy focus

### Current Status (as of 2024)
- CEO of Telegram
- Estimated net worth: ~$15 billion
- Lives in Dubai, UAE
- French and UAE citizenship

## Philosophy and Worldview

### Core Beliefs
- Strong libertarian tendencies
- Anti-censorship
- Privacy advocacy
- Technology as liberating force
- "I fear not new ideas, but old ones" (quote from Durov)

### On Transparency
- Advocated for personal/business transparency
- Believed in "not lying" as key to avoiding manipulation

### On Money
- Reportedly indifferent to personal wealth
- More motivated by ideals and building products

## Quotes and Statements

- "I fear not new ideas, but old ones" (Я боюсь не новых идей, а старых)

- Used Latin motto from Faust: "Vi veri universum vivus vici" - "[By the power of] truth, I, while living, have conquered the universe"
