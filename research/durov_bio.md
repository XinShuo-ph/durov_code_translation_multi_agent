# Pavel Durov Biography

## Early Life
- **Born**: October 10, 1984, Leningrad (St. Petersburg), USSR.
- **Family**:
    - Father: Valery Durov, Doctor of Philological Sciences, head of Classical Philology at St. Petersburg State University (SPbGU).
    - Mother: Albina Durova.
    - Brother: Nikolai Durov (b. 1980), mathematician, coding genius, 2-time ICPC world champion.
- **Childhood**: Lived in Turin, Italy, for several years while father taught there. Returned to Russia in early 90s.
- **Education**: Academic Gymnasium (AG) in St. Petersburg; graduated from SPbGU (English Philology) with honors (Red Diploma).

## Pre-VK Projects
- **Durov.com**: Library of exam answers for humanities students.
- **SPBGU.ru**: Forum for university students, known for anonymity and intellectual debates. Durov famously argued with himself using different accounts to spark engagement.

## VKontakte Era (2006-2014)
- **Founding**: 2006, inspired by Facebook. Co-founders: Vyacheslav Mirilashvili, Lev Leviev.
- **Role**: CEO, Product Architect, Ideologist.
- **Philosophy**: "Totem" leadership, libertarianism, free speech.
- **Conflict**: 2011 elections (refused to block opposition groups), 2013 "white knight" investor UCP, 2014 forced resignation.

## Post-VK (Telegram)
- Left Russia in April 2014.
- Founded Telegram with Nikolai Durov.
- Focus on privacy, encryption, and speed.
- Citizenship: St. Kitts and Nevis, France, UAE.
