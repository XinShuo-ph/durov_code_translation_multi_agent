# Pavel Durov Biography

## Basic Information
- **Full Name**: Pavel Valeryevich Durov (Павел Валерьевич Дуров)
- **Born**: October 10, 1984, Leningrad (now St. Petersburg), USSR
- **Nationality**: Russian (later obtained citizenship of Saint Kitts and Nevis, France, UAE)
- **Known For**: Founder of VKontakte (VK), Founder/CEO of Telegram

---

## Family Background

### Parents
- **Father**: Valery Semyonovich Durov - Professor of philology (Roman languages specialist)
  - Academic at St. Petersburg State University
  - Published scholar in philology
- **Mother**: Albina Durova - Later worked as a realtor
  - Helped Pavel buy properties after VK success

### Siblings
- **Nikolai Durov** (older brother) - Mathematician and programmer
  - Math prodigy, read advanced books at age 3
  - Multiple gold medals at International Mathematical Olympiad (1996, 1997, 1998)
  - Ph.D. in mathematics from University of Bonn
  - Key technical architect behind VKontakte and Telegram
  - Created VK's core algorithms and infrastructure
  
- **Mikhail Durov** (oldest, half-brother from father's side)
  - Lived separately from the family
  - Less involved in Pavel's ventures

## Childhood and Education

### Early Years (1984-1991)
- Born in Leningrad during late Soviet era
- Lived in Italy briefly while father taught abroad
- Returned to Russia after USSR collapse

### Childhood in St. Petersburg (1991-2001)
- Family lived in новостройки (new housing developments) in outskirts
  - Neighborhood near marshland and Baltic Sea
  - Rough area with "гопники" (street thugs)
- Describes himself as isolated, bookish child
  - Read Cervantes and other classics
  - Poor eyesight, speech impediment (logopedia issues)
  - Spent time exploring abandoned military infrastructure
- Influenced by brother Nikolai's intellectual achievements

### Education
- **Secondary School**: Academic Gymnasium No. 56 (Академическая гимназия №56)
  - One of best schools in St. Petersburg
  - Strong in philology and humanities
  
- **University**: St. Petersburg State University
  - Faculty of Philology (Филологический факультет)
  - Degree in philology (graduated 2006)
  - Won Potanin Foundation scholarship 3 times consecutively (rare achievement)
  - Started student forum spbgu.ru during studies

## Early Ventures (2001-2006)

### Durov.com
- Personal website and early experiments
- "Durov.com Club" - exclusive network for students
- Black business cards: "Pavel Durov, President"

### Student Forum (spbgu.ru)
- Created forum for St. Petersburg State University students
- Became popular platform for student communication
- Precursor to VKontakte

### Olymp Beauty Contest
- Co-organized with Ilya Perekopsky at university
- Demonstrated organizational and marketing skills
- Built network of contacts at philology faculty

---

## VKontakte Era (2006-2014)

### Founding (2006)
- Launched VKontakte on October 10, 2006 (his birthday)
- Initially exclusive to students (required university email)
- Quickly expanded to general public
- Modeled on Facebook but adapted for Russian market

### Growth Period (2006-2012)
- Rapid user growth: 100+ million users by 2012
- Became dominant social network in Russia and CIS
- Key features: music sharing, video, messaging, groups
- Known for tolerating pirated content (attracted users but created legal issues)

### Key Partners and Team
- **Ilya Perekopsky** - Vice President, early partner from university
- **Nikolai Durov** - Chief technology architect
- **Vyacheslav Mirilashvili** - Early investor (10% stake)
- **Lev Leviev** - Early investor (10% stake)

### Investor Relations
- **2007**: Yuri Milner's DST invested (Digital Sky Technologies)
- **2010-2011**: Mail.ru Group acquired stakes from various shareholders
- Growing tensions with corporate shareholders

### Departure (2014)
- April 2014: Announced resignation as CEO
- Cited pressure from Russian authorities and shareholders
- Sold remaining shares and left Russia
- Stated he wouldn't return under current political circumstances

---

## Telegram Era (2013-Present)

### Founding Telegram (2013)
- Launched August 2013 as secure messaging app
- Developed by Nikolai Durov and team
- Focus on encryption, speed, and privacy
- Headquartered in Dubai, UAE (later officially registered in British Virgin Islands)

### Philosophy
- Strong anti-censorship stance
- Commitment to user privacy
- Resistance to government backdoors
- "Digital exile" from Russia

### Key Events
- **2018**: Russian government banned Telegram (later lifted in 2020)
- **2020**: Telegram reached 400+ million users
- **2024**: 900+ million monthly active users
