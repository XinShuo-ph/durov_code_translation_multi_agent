## Style Notes

### Russian → English
- Use American English spelling (Stanford context)
- Preserve Durov's sharp, direct voice
- Tech terms: use industry-standard translations
- Names: standard English transliteration

### Russian → 中文
- Use Simplified Chinese (Mainland)
- Internet slang: adapt to Chinese netizen culture (微博/知乎 style)
- Names: standard Xinhua transliteration for Russian names
- Tech terms: use established translations

### Russian → 日本語
- Balance formal/informal register to match original
- Names: katakana for foreign names
- Consider Japanese tech/startup culture parallels
- Appropriate use of honorifics based on context
