## Style Guidelines

### Voice and Tone
- **Russian original**: Sharp, intellectual, sometimes provocative
- **English**: Maintain sharpness, use tech industry register
- **Chinese**: Adapt to 互联网文化 (internet culture), maintain intellectual tone
- **Japanese**: Balance formal/informal, preserve provocative edge

### Consistency Rules
1. Always use the translations in this glossary
2. Keep company names in original form (VKontakte, Mail.ru)
3. Use standard transliterations for Russian names
4. Adapt idioms appropriately, don't translate literally
5. Add translator notes for culture-specific references

- **Russian**: Black
- **English**: Blue
- **Chinese**: Red
- **Japanese**: Green
