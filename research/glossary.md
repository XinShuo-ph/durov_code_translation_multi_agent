## Style Guidelines

### Russian Names
- Use full romanization first, then short form
- Example: "Pavel Valerievich Durov (hereinafter 'Pavel' or 'Durov')"

### Titles and Honorifics
- Omit patronymics after first mention
- Use "Mr." only in formal contexts

### Units and Numbers
- Keep metric system
- Dates: Use target language conventions

### VK Feature Names
- Keep "VKontakte" for brand
- Translate feature names contextually
